My Passion

Why I Am On The Dyslex.ie Team ?
January 2016 - Present Date

As a child, one of my bestfriends suffered from dyslexia. Simple everyday tasks such as reading and writing were a burden hindering him from achieving his full potential. I noticed he had to work twice as hard to achieve similar grades if not less than someone without dyslexia. As a result he experienced great embarrassment from his poor grades and reading ability having a huge effect on his self-esteem which affected his personal development and well-being. My friend is like the many other dyslexic people around the world where their dyslexia is not bad enough to obtain additional support from the government yet are still greatly hindered by dyslexia. All this became too overwhelming for my friend and one day in school I noticed a complete change of attitude in my friend. What was once a happy, hardworking student was now a timide and lacking drive in life as if he has given up, which is a parents worst nightmare. I never felt so useless in my life there was nothing I could do to help. I never want another person go through what my friend went through. I began to research more about dyslexia and discovered that making adjustments to the page according to the dyslexic person's needs could greatly improve their reading ability. I decided to implement this with technology and Dyslex.ie was born.
