ABOUT THE COMPANY

Key features of our Dyslex.ie

Dyslex.ie is an award winning chrome extension that helps the dyslexic community improve their reading ability by making websites more accessible

Design

We have designed software tailored to your needs with a simple intial survey. This determines the modifications that best fit you. Modifications consist of changing the format of the website such as background colour,... font style, line-spacing, character-spacing and much more. We don't stop there, we also provide additional reading tool developed by you, the users, that will help you read at ease

Passion

Dyslexia, one in ten people are affected by it in the world today and yet it still remain neglected with a lack of resources, particularly in the technology sector. This has a major effect on the dyslexic community's life as this ... prevents them from achieving their full potential in life. Dyslex.ie we want to tackle this problem head on.

Technology

Technology is everywhere in our pockets, cars and even our homes. They're making our lives easier and more exciting. However, in the world today there is a lack of online tools for the dyslexic comminity. Dyslex.ie is here to fill ... this void by using the latest technologies to insure you are achieving your maximimium potential.

Support

Dyslex.ie is always here to support you. Our software is here to support your reading needs and our dedicated Dyslex.ie team will be happy deal with any other issue that may araise. Your feedback is essential to the developement of ... Dyslex.ie so feel free to contact us.
